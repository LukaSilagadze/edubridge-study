Frequently Asked Questions

Certificates can only be uploaded on our platform by registered users. After the activity is completed, the certificate is automatically added to your profile.

To register, select the desired activity on the "Discover Activities" page, click the "Register" button, and fill in the required information.

To share your certificates, go to the "Certificates" page, select the desired certificate, and click the "Share" button. You can share it on social media or via email.

To edit your profile, go to the "My Profile" page. Click the "Edit Profile" button, make the necessary changes, and save your updates.

EduBridge is a platform that connects students to school activities and helps them document their successes.
